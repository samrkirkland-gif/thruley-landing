Everything for your first week, ready to post. Download to your phone, upload to Instagram, and add a trending sound (options are in your email).

How to use

  1. Download — tap a video, then Share → Save Video (or grab everything with the button below).
  2. Upload natively in Instagram — not through Buffer, so you can add trending audio.
  3. Add a trending sound — pick from the 10 options per post in your email. Look for the ↗ trending arrow.
  4. Post in order — logo reveal → coming-soon teaser → carousel.
